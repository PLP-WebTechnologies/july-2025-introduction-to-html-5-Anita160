About Kenya Scouts

The Kenya Scouts Association is part of the global Scouting movement, dedicated to empowering young people to become responsible citizens. We focus on community service, leadership, and outdoor education, helping young people build life skills and values.

Our Programs

Cubs Section (Ages 6-11)

Fun activities and games that introduce children to teamwork, creativity, and community values.

Scouts Section (Ages 12-18)

Leadership training, outdoor adventures, and skill-building to prepare young people for life's challenges.

Rovers Section (Ages 18-26)

Community service projects, mentoring, and advanced leadership opportunities for young adults.
